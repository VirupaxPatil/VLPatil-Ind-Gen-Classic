### Typical Parts List – 5 kW VLPatil-Ind-Gen-Classic (2025 India prices)

- **Main Induction Generator (MIG)**  
  5 kW, 4-pole, wound-rotor, 415 V, 1500 rpm (e.g. Kirloskar / Crompton / Bharat Bijlee old stock)  
  ≈ ₹38,000 – ₹48,000

- **Auxiliary Induction Machine (AIM)**  
  1.5–2 kW, 4-pole or 6-pole, wound-rotor, 415 V (any old slip-ring motor)  
  ≈ ₹18,000 – ₹25,000

- **Servo system for AIM stator rotation**  
  400–750 W BLDC motor + gearbox or VFD-driven induction motor + simple controller  
  ≈ ₹12,000 – ₹18,000

- **Low-power slip-ring set for AIM stator**  
  3-phase, 5–10 A (easily available in any industrial market)  
  ≈ ₹4,000 – ₹7,000

- **Mechanical cradle, shaft coupling, base frame**  
  Local fabrication (mild steel + bearings)  
  ≈ ₹25,000 – ₹35,000

**Total estimated cost (2025)**: ₹1.1 – ₹1.35 lakh for the generator unit  
(plus tower + blades = complete system ≈ ₹2.6 – ₹3.4 lakh)

All parts available in any Tier-2 Indian city. No imported electronics.
